About YE

Kanye West, now known as YE, is one of the most influential artists of our generation. With over 160 million records sold worldwide and 24 Grammy Awards, YE has shaped modern hip-hop and popular culture like no other.

From groundbreaking albums like "The College Dropout" and "My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy" to cultural phenomena like "Yeezus" and "The Life of Pablo", YE has consistently pushed boundaries and redefined what it means to be an artist.

This exclusive performance at GelreDome marks his triumphant return to Europe after more than a decade. Expect an unforgettable night featuring classics, deep cuts and surprises from one of music's greatest innovators.
